# ACP Case Studies

This page collects short, anonymized adoption examples for ACP.

## Hiring
- A recruiting team used `agreement_v1`, `revision_v1`, and `verification_report_v1` to separate candidate review, final approval, and post-decision audit notes.
- Result: handoffs became easier to explain internally, and review artifacts stayed attached to the original agreement boundary.

## Outsourced production
- A small agency project used `agreement_v1`, `delegation_edge_v1`, `settlement_intent_v1`, and `verification_report_v1` to keep client instructions separate from delivery revisions.
- Result: revision scope and payment intent were easier to track across multiple handoffs.

## Research
- An investigation workflow used `agreement_v1`, `evidence_pack_v1`, and `verification_report_v1` to keep claims tied to evidence.
- Result: reviewers could trace conclusions back to source material without reconstructing the process from chat logs.

## Marketing
- A campaign team used `agreement_v1`, `event_v1`, and `settlement_intent_v1` to record who approved launch and who authorized follow-up actions.
- Result: incident review after a campaign change was faster because the approval boundary was explicit.

## Internal multi-agent
- An internal agent workflow used `agreement_v1`, `delegation_edge_v1`, `resource_reservation_v1`, and `freeze_record_v1` to constrain child-agent work and stop unsafe propagation.
- Result: the team could freeze a problematic branch without losing the parent audit trail.

## Notes
- All examples are anonymized and intentionally short.
- These are reference patterns, not universal policy templates.
