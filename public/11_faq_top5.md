# ACP FAQ (Top 5)

## Q1. What is ACP?
ACP is an accountability layer for delegated AI work.

## Q2. What is ACP not?
ACP is not an execution framework, not a transport protocol, and not a payment rail.

## Q3. Why is prompt not contract?
A prompt is instruction text. ACP contract boundary is revision-bound, signed, and replay-auditable.

## Q4. Why is claim not evidence?
Claims become evaluable only when bound to evidence artifacts and checked under explicit verification rules.

## Q5. Why is verification not settlement?
Verification determines integrity/acceptance. Settlement determines release/hold/refund/block. They are separate protocol concerns.
