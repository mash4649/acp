# ACP Public Package Baseline

- ACP is an accountability layer for delegated AI work.
- ACP is not an execution framework, transport protocol, or payment rail.
- Keep the three separations explicit: prompt != contract, claim != evidence, verification != settlement.
- Keep all external messaging aligned to Core-15 and replayable accountability.
- Avoid broad replacement claims against MCP/A2A/workflow engines/payment rails.

# ACP 公開パッケージ基準（日本語補助）

- ACPは委任AI作業のための説明責任レイヤ。
- 実行基盤/通信規格/決済レールの置き換えを主張しない。
- `prompt != contract`、`claim != evidence`、`verification != settlement` を必ず分離する。
- Core-15 と再現可能な検証可能性に整合させる。
